Your options for making international phone calls:

1. Messaging apps or Wi-Fi Calling.
2. Roaming

## Wi-Fi Calling

1. Turn on Wi-Fi Calling on your phone
2. Connect to a Wi-Fi network. The phone carrier shown will suffix with "Wi-Fi"
3. (Optional) Turn on Airplane mode to avoid roaming charges
4. Dial the number as you would normally (even when abroad)
