# ✈️ Flying

Everything about flying.

## Platforms

- [Google Flight](https://www.google.com/flights/)
- [Skyscanner](https://www.skyscanner.com/)
- [KAYAK](https://www.kayak.com/)
- [Kiwi.com](https://www.kiwi.com/)
- [Going](https://www.going.com/): Flight deal alert based on crawled Google Flight data
- [Priceline](https://www.priceline.com/): Offer "Express Deal" which are slightly cheaper but don't know exact itinerary until booked.
- [Navan](https://navan.com/): Corporate travel platform but also do leisure

## Point Award Tickets

- [PointsYeah](https://www.pointsyeah.com/): Most powerful for free user.
- [Point.me](https://www.point.me/): Paid-only, [AMEX edition](https://amex.point.me/) is free to all members.
- [Roame](https://roame.travel/)
- [AwardHacker](https://www.awardhacker.com/): Search routes, but can't search for specific dates.
- [Seats.aero](https://seats.aero/): Free for flights within 60 days, Paid member can search up to 1 year.
- [ExpertFlyer](https://www.expertflyer.com/): Seat Upgrade alert
